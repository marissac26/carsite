Iron & Steele

Traditional Hot Rods and Custom Roadsters

Iron and Steele is the brainchild of Jake Steele; a car builder, author and automotive historian based out of Portland, Oregon. Jake has been a staple in the hot rod community, founding The Estranged Car Club somewhere along the way. Seeing the need for a web-based platform for people to gather to read about traditional hot rods, the history of the phenomenon and the people involved, Iron and Steele was born. It is a collaboration with a multitude of like-minded individuals involved with the greatest hobby and passion on earth: Hot rods. It is meant to cater to not only the die-hard car guys, but also to invite people from all walks of life to see what all the fuss is about with these old cars through amazing photography, and creative story telling

1931 Model A Coupe:

  • A period-perfect representation of a true 1930's / 1940's hot rod with a 5" chop
  • 3" dropped axle in the front, split wish bones
  • model a block, bored .040 over
  • single Stromberg 97 downdraft carburetor and pressurized fuel system
  • custom made exhaust manifold mated to a baffled torque-tube exhaust
