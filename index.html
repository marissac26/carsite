Iron & Steele

Traditional Hot Rods and Customs

Iron and Steele is the brainchild of Jake Steele; a car builder, author and automotive historian based out of Portland, Oregon. Jake has been a staple in the hot rod community in the Northwest and beyond for years, founding The Estranged Car Club somewhere along the way. Seeing the need for a web-based platform for people to gather to read about traditional hot rods, the history of the phenomenon and the people involved, Iron and Steele was born. It is a collaboration with a multitude of like-minded individuals involved with the greatest hobby and passion on earth: Hot rods. It is meant to cater to not only the die-hard car guys, but also to invite people from all walks of life to see what all the fuss is about with these old cars through amazing photography, and creative story telling
